PERSON OF THE WEEK

Cassidy Williams (@cassidoo) is a Principal Developer Experience Engineer at Netlify. She has worked at React Training, CodePen, Amazon, L4 Digital, Clarifai, and Venmo and is one of Glamour Magazine’s 35 Women Under 35 Changing the Tech Industry and LinkedIn’s Top Professionals 35 & Under. As an avid speaker, Cassidy has participated in several events including the Grace Hopper Celebration for Women in Computing, TEDx, the United Nations, and the U.S.A. Science and Engineering Festival. Cassidy runs a weekly newsletter and loves teaching and helping people become better coders. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Cassidy!
